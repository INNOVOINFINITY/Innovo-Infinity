About

With the thinking - “more events = more experience = more fun ….”,

Our club conducted another yet amazing as well as exciting event – _“Ninja Wave”_.
We are pleased to share with you that this time, our club event was collaborated with: Coding Ninjas

More about Contest

Coding Ninjas is an excellent platform where u can -Learn new courses, code using variety of programming languages as well as upskill your capabilities as well as your resume...
The duration of this event was 90 Minutes. The event comprised of:

• Aptitude section
• Reasoning section
• Coding section

Number of students who participated : 151

On the basis of performance of each participant, ranks were allotted to all the participants :) Several prizes were distributed such as:

• Amazon vouchers
• Discount coupons
• Participation Certificates

And much more ….
Top 5 ranks were given Amazon vouchers as well heavy discount on courses. Also rewards up to Rs.90,000 were distributed. Discount vouchers up to RS.1500/- were given to top 80 Ranked participants on all courses offered by them.
