What We Do?

Development

We have gotten a fantastic start to the club website, and we have many projects on the horizon! Our next major effort will be redesigning a website for a club on campus and continuing to improve our own site.

Programming

The Competitive Programming Club is about doing ridiculously difficult problems within unfairly short time limits. Our focus is To improve the ability of our members in implementing algorithms using programming languages such as C++ and Java.

TECHTALKS has been organized by the club , which is an innovative and successful learning practice that was introduced to provide knowledge dissemination platform for our students.

Webinars

We Host various webinars/Hands-on workshops which helps the students to learn various new technical skills trending in the field of technology . Our utmost aim is to reach every student and provide them the best assistance from our side which will help them grow and stay updated.
